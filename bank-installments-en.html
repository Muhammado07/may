Here's how it works

Step One

During checkout, please pay the total order amount with your credit card.

Step Two

Contact your bank to check if you are eligible for the installment program.

Step Three

Ask your bank to convert your purchase amount to your chosen installment plan.

Bank installment programs are payment methods offered by partnered banks with YORK to enable a customer to convert the value of a paid order into an installment payment plan according to the plan that fits their needs as long as the purchase value exceed 1000 SAR and the credit card used for payment is issued from one of the following banks:
